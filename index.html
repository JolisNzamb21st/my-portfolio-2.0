Child Immunization Management System with SMS Reminder

This system enables health facilities to efficiently manage child immunization programs by digitizing patient records and vaccine schedules. The platform allows nurses to register children, track upcoming and overdue immunizations, and generate real-time updates on each child’s vaccination status. A built-in SMS module sends automated reminders to parents ahead of each appointment, reducing missed vaccinations and improving health outcomes.The system enhances reliability, organization, and communication within low-resource settings.
